Sections

 

Joey Scouts (6 - 8 yrs)

Joey Scouts is your first section in Scouts. It’s for boys and girls aged 6 (5 if attending Year 1 at school) to 8 and it’s all about fun!

Cub Scouts (8 - 11 yrs)

Cub Scouts is the second section into Scouts. It’s for boys and girls aged 8 to 11 and it’s all about adventure!

Scouts (11 - 15 yrs)

Scouts is for boys and girls aged 11 to 15 and is the most well known section of the Scouting Movement – Scouts is where the action is!

Venturer Scouts (15 - 18 yrs)

Venturer Scouts is for males and females aged 15 to their 18th birthday (or if still attending school, until the end of the year in which they turn 18) who never turn down a good challenge and love to push themselves to their limits

Rover Scouts (18 - 25)

Rover Scouts is for males and females aged 18 until their 26th birthday who love socialising, challenging themselves, and seizing once-in-a-lifetime opportunities!
