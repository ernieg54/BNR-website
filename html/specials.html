70th Anniversary of The Commonwealth

2019 is the 70th anniversary of the modern Commonwealth. Each year, a new Commonwealth theme is chosen and announced on Commonwealth Day. The theme becomes the focus for activities undertaken during that year throughout the Commonwealth.

The 2019 Commonwealth theme 'A Connected Commonwealth' highlights the ongoing global engagement made possible as our Commonwealth family of nations work together in friendship

Commonwealth Nations work together to protect the natural environment and the oceans. So many of our Commonwealth nations are connected shore-to-shore. The Commonwealth Games encourage all young people to participate in sport for peace and friendship. Commonwealth Nations and Commonwealth organisations encourage empowerment and inclusion for all people - particularly women, young people and developing communities.
