Implementing the New Youth Program in Brisbane North Region

As the largest Region in Queensland, Brisbane North has introduced an initiative to coordinate the New Youth Program implementation within the Region.

The intent of this initiative is to promote the Queensland New Youth Program Implementation (QYIP) by providing education and facilitation support throughout the Region. This is primarily to remove and transfer some of the burden of providing this support from the Branch team to our Region.

Four key people have been identified at Region level as subject matter experts. They are:

  • Hsien-Jin
  • Russell
  • Emma
  • Jenny

All requests for support, workshops, information sessions, etc. should be directed to one of these people.
