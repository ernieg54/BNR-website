Implementing the New Youth Program in Brisbane North Region

We would like to introduce the Bunyips, a Brisbane North Region initiative to promote and coordinate the Queensland New Youth Program Implementation (QYIP) within the Region.

As the largest Region in Queensland, in terms of both Group and youth numbers, the intent is primarily to transfer some of the burden of implementing the New Youth Program from the Branch team to our Region.

There is no special Brisbane North implementation, as such, but the Bunyips (or BNYIP if you really want an acronym,) should be the first contact choice for information, implementation support, workshops, training, etc.

The principal Bunyips are Hsien-Jin, Russell, Jenny and Emma, but most of the Region team members are well-trained in QYIP and able to provide support and advice.

A dedicated email 'hotline' has been created to get in touch with the Bunyips.
