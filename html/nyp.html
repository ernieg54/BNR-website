Implementing the New Youth Program in Brisbane North Region

The Brisbane North Region supports the New Youth Program implementation. With the full endorsement of the Branch team, we are providing additional support to help our Groups and Leaders to successfully implement the new program.

Our NYP implementation support team consists of Hsien-Jin, Russell, Jenny and Emma. They should be used as the first point of contact for all requests within the Region for information, implementation support, workshops, training, etc.

A special email 'hotline' will be created to reach them.
