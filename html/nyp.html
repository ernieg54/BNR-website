Implementing the New Youth Program in Brisbane North Region

As the largest Region in Queensland, Brisbane North has introduced an initiative to promote and coordinate the Queensland New Youth Program Implementation (QYIP) within the Region.

The intent is primarily to transfer some of the burden of providing this support from the Branch team to our Region.

Four people have been identified at Region level as key resources for the New Youth Program Implementation. They are:

  • Hsien-Jin Teoh, ARC Adult Training and Development
  • Russell Davie, ARC Adventurous Activities
  • Jenny Annand, ARC Group Support
  • Emma Storie, Region Leader

All requests for information, implementation support, workshops, training, etc. should be directed to one of these people in the first instance.

The ARCs for Cub Scouts, Scouts and Venturer Scouts have also each received OAS training and can provide support within these Sections.
